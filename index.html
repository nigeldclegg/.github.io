About Project Midlife Crisis

I'm Nigel, and I'm on a journey to explore life's "what ifs." Traveling in my self-converted campervan, Big Yellow, and with my motorbike, I'm finding out what it means to live a life of true value, where the greatest experiences often come at zero financial cost. This is a project about finding freedom, hidden gems, and a new way of living in a world obsessed with profit.
